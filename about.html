ABOUT

Hi, I’m Sherrie Fay Lin.

I love tangerines and toast.

Oh, and designing things

In this world where everything is connected and anything goes,

I find myself striving to dive deeper in any form of design that catches my eye

I started with designing heat press t-shirts with Adobe Illustrator CS3 10 years ago,

Then found an unexpected side-passion in film while by iMovie to take all my jr. high school projects to a ridiculously extra level.

This all, slowly but surely, escalated to the rest Adobe Creative Suite, 2D/3D software, prototyping, model-making, code, and now an ongoing list of whatever sparks my mind.

You see, I believe the world of design is a glass room. It’s hard to say why, but sometimes you just feel stuck in the same fonts, compositions, colors, and walls.

I find myself running in circles in there all the time.

But when you combine two or three or 10 different worlds, it’s like opening a bunch of unseen doors that let you breathe in new perspective, concepts, and ideas.

It creates something extraordinary.

I’m still looking for some doors,

but that’s the path I want to climb as a designer.
