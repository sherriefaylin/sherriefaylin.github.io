RUBBISH

AN APP FOR THE TRAVELERS, THE HANDICAPPED, THE SENIORS.

FEBRUARY 2018

MERN STACK SERVICE APP

ROLE: FRONT-END DEVELOPER, UI/UX DESIGNER


INTRO

Have you ever forgotten to pull your trash bins out on garbage day? Or left town and had no choice but to leave your trash bins to stink up your home for days? Or just didn’t want to take out your trash? RUBBISH is here to help! While encouraging some good ol’ neighbor to neighbor relationships, RUBBISH connects you to trustworthy volunteers down the street to help you out on a rare occasion or daily basis.

SIGN UP

To start, a user must sign up- however, we kept the database minimal, storing only relevant information and user location when allowed.

WALK THROUGH

Starting from the home page, you have the navigation dial.

THE WALL AND POSTS

The “home page” would be the wall. Here, there are two tabs to alternate back and forth between “post” and “wall”. The post is a simple form if the user wanted to post on the wall. The wall is compiled of posts from users within a few miles radius of your user location. You can adjust the distance with the “edit” button in the top right corner.
If you click on a post, you will be prompted with an alert, asking if you would like to connect with that user. If you confirm, a 1:1 message will be opened. Here, you can contact that person and organize the service personally.

MESSAGING

This brings us to the messages. From the newly opened connection or from the home navigation dial, you can see all your messages in the app.

USER PROFILE

Each user can access and edit their profile through the option in the main navigation dial. This app stores only the most necessary information, so it is minimal. From here, you can also logout of the app.
